History of the FIFA World Cup


Analysing Trends in Goals Scored and Average Attendance per World Cup

Visualisation of FIFA World cup statistics collected by fans and collated into an open-source kaggle dataset.
This Dataset provides a breakdown of all World Cups held in the period 1930 - 2014 and tracks stats such as goals scored and match attendance
Additionally provides a breakdown of all tournament podium finishes per edition of the World cup.

Analysing trends in Goal Scored across each edition



As per the visualisation above, it can be observed that the number of goals scored per edition of the FIFA World cup has followed an overall linear trend.
Large Spikes can be seen in editions where the number of matches has increased.
Drops in goals scored can be attributed to the changing nature and style of the game in response to new mentalities demonstrated by individual players and teams.

Average Attendance per World Cup Edition


The visualisation above seeks to visualise the average Tournament attendance per edition of the world cup.
The drops in attendance can be attributed to significantexternal events such as the World Wars
